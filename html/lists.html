Ordered List

  1. First, you will need to invest time, research, and empathy in an issue or a cause that many may not be aware of.
  2. Next, you will need to envision and name an appropriate event: walk, race, restaurant crawl, silent auction, ball, etc. Thinking creatively will add value to your portfolio.
  3. Lastly, the final output will be a responsive website in the promotion of the fund raising event to raise money and awareness for the cause or issue.

  • One appropriate interactive feature with jQuery
